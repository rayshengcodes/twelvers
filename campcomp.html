Chairs

A heist is only as successful as its ringleaders. You might have seen them in movies: they could be Vito Corleone, they could be Al Capone or they could be the boss of a Chicago street syndicate. They have ears everywhere and eyes anywhere and they're always watching. From the shadows, they've contrive the XII Heist to where it is today, a family that has yet to be caught, a family that is always on the move looking for it's next gig. They're the ones who make sure things are in place and are always one step ahead.
