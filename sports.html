Hall XII Sports

Our Sports

Powered by our Hall 12 Sports Directors, our sports team have been putting stellar performances in the Inter-Hall Games, most notably with the hockey girls making it to the Semi Finals for the first time in XII's history in 2019.

Being a hall with a strong heritage, there is a strong sense of continuity and camaraderie amongst the sports teams. We are actively looking to recruit new players who are able to contribute and become part of Hall 12. Competitions aside, IHG is about allowing all hall residents to be able to participate in a variety of sports and all residents are welcome to join any of Hall 12's sports teams irregardless of experience.

If you have an interest in joining these activities in Hall 12, stay tuned for the sign up links! We hope to hear from you!
