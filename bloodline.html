Red Blood

Across the seven seas, near the valley of Mount NIE and deep within the taverns of the greatest of all halls lies the ever resting fomododragons of Red Blood. Legend has it that Red Blood are the wildest people to be around. Ever since the pandemic happened, only the wholesomerestest remained. Dont try to leave us out as FOMO courses through our veins.

Do you always find yourself hungry at 630 and unsure of who to eat with? Red Blood will ALWAYS be here for you with weekly dinner dates! Don't forget your cups at supper sessions as tea is bound to be spilt. So what are you waiting for? Come FOMO with us now!

Spectre

Green Blood

“The world does not revolve around you” and yet, our blood revolves around everyone in it! From chilling in someone’s room, having meals or going out for supper together, to attending hall events in full force and flexing our high attendance rate, to even meeting outside of hall for celebrations such as Chinese New Year, there’s never a dull moment in Green Blood! With our heritage in spending quality time together across the notion of “senior and junior”, we do our best to include everyone :) We consist of many different individuals from different backgrounds and yet, these factors do not define us.

It’s not about eyeing top spot for everything - despite us winning Best Performance in Orientation a few times in a row, winning the Inter-OG Telematch, Best Group Leader and Best Programmer, Hall King, Hall Queen, JCRC president 3 years in a row already. At the end of the day, walking away with solid friends and memorable moments are better than any tangible trophy. The reliable friends who can always be turned to for laughs or help are a better prize any day. One can say “aiya, stay hall for what? Waste time”. However, it’s never a waste of time in Green Blood! As long as you have made one good memory here, it would be one for life. The friendships forged, to the questionable memorable moments in and out of hall, Green Blood Best Blood!

“You can’t have Hall XII’s colour without Green”


Sterling

Blue Blood

Despite being a bunch of people with different personalities, we embrace each other’s endearing little quirks and come together as one. Though you may often find us more laid-back when it comes to activities, but we're just having fun and we do have our competitive side that shines through once in a blue moon!! Ultimately, it is the moments spent chilling together and our carefreeness that forged our friendships.

Not to forget our frequent birthday celebrations every month with A VERY SPECIAL TRADITIONAL GIFT and also random suppers late in the night (Steamboats, Macs, Raydy) which is free when someone feels like it!

Chill like an ice, go with the flow.


Hydra

Purple Blood

30 Nanyang Crescent is not so far when you have a home away from home. Purple Blood is a one big wholesome family. We always have a great time together, alcohol-free (mostly). We are an inclusive family, and you’ll always have a seat at the mahjong table with our Mahjong Queen. After a long day of lectures and tutorials, you can look forward to standing around in a big group at awkward locations, taking forever to decide on things. To study, to eat, or to chill. Regardless, we know how to have a good time, and you’ll always have purple blood fam around at our social events in hall. Work hard, play harder.

No matter who you are and where you come from, as long as you are up for it, purple blood welcomes you! You can join our Arc club for amazing study/tiktok seshs. If you ever feel like a plastic bag, drifting through the wind, wanting to start again, Purple blood is for you.

If you are meant to be with us, you are meant to be, TRUST.


Sherlock

Grey Blood

Scared to be stuck and alone in Pulau NTU? Grey Braaad is here for you! Transition to uni life can be tough, but it doesn’t have to be with our wholesome and unlimited room crashing passes, supper parties and study sessions. When school gets intense, heart to heart talks and mahjong sessions with Grey Braaad helps you get through it!

Grey Braaad promises a myriad of colourful and quirky personalities that will suit your different needs. As an inclusive blood, we promote inter-blood interactions through hall activities and friendly gossip. Uni life can be busy and hectic and maintaining a social life might be hard at times. However, when you join us, you’ll always be one of us. Grey blood, a home away from home.

Way up high, or down low, Grey Blood is here wherever u go.
