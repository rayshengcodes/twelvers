Hall XII Culture

The Social Hall

Here in Hall 12, while we are widely known to be a wild and alcoholic hall, in actual fact we do have the artsy, sporty and brainy part of us. Ran by a team of committed hall individuals, also known as JCRC (Junior Common Room Committee), they establish our holistic and cultural history.

Our dance, cheer, jam band, sports and recreational games have achieved outstanding results in competitions NTU-wide. This includes the Inter-Hall Cheerleading Competition (IHCC), Hall Olympiad Closing Ceremony (HOCC), Inter-Hall Games (IHG) and Inter-Hall Recreational Games (IHRG).

Hall 12 currently accommodates around 600 students. Our resident population comprises a diverse range of students in terms of both nationality and abilities. We are thus able to pick up new culture and skills through interaction during the numerous hall activities. Such activities include, Inter-Hall Games, Exam Suppers, Culture Night, Hall Dinner and Dance, Freshmen Orientation Camps and many more! This has led us to becoming one of the more vibrant places to be.

Don’t all those activities and achievements sound exciting? If you have an interest in contributing to Hall 12, do drop us your contacts! We hope to hear from you!

Residents and alumni, do feel free to share this page with your fellow alumni, you will be kept in the loop for updates!

Douzers

DOUZERS is Hall 12's cheerleading team and we live by the motto of "Just do your best, your friends will do the rest.”

Representing Hall 12 in the annual Inter-Hall Cheerleading Competition (IHCC), Douzzers have been consistently acheiving stellar results in the interhall competition, making a Hall 12 a strong name within the cheer community. Other than participating in IHCC, DOUZERS has also competed in the Asia Cheer Invitational Championship (ACIC) 2019 where we clinched 2nd Runner-Up in the Double Group Stunt Co-Ed Advanced Category.

But outside of cheer, DOUZERS is not just a team. We’re also a family with multiple jb day trips, dmj, hdj & ssh sessions! (Join us to know what these stand for 😉)
See you next season for more of DOU-ZERS, HALL TWELVE, DOUZERS, LET’S GO!

Jam Band

Love music? Love performing? Bad at coming up with band names? Then Hall 12 Jam Band is the place to be! Singing, keyboard, guitar, bass, drums, violin, egg shaker; you name it, we do it!

We jam and chill in our cozy but not very soundproof Jamband Room... And we love it when the security guard occasionally pops up to join in the fun :) Then when the opportunity comes along, we showcase our passion and talent at performances in hall, school and sometimes even outside NTU!

So come join us if you share our passion to make music and bond with fellow music-lovers over practices and chill sesh-es :D Looking forward to having you on board!!
